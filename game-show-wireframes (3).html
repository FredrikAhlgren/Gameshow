📝 Design Notes - Judge Panel

  • ✅ Three question types: Standard Q&A, Closest Number, Creative/Funny
  • ✅ Standard: Traditional trivia with exact answer
  • ✅ Closest Number: Teams guess a number, closest wins (e.g., "How many jelly beans?")
  • ✅ Creative/Funny: Judge picks the best/funniest answer (e.g., "Things you don't want to hear at doctor's")
  • ✅ Question & Category setup - customize all 25 questions and 5 category names
  • ✅ Team picture upload - add custom photos for each team
  • ✅ Support for 2-8 teams with color indicators
  • ✅ Partial credit slider for nuanced scoring (0-100%)
  • ✅ Answer history log tracks all responses and results
